WARREN SPORTS & FITNESS

The Warren Sports & Fitness Ministry (WSF) focuses on caring for the WHOLE person – emotionally, mentally, physically, and spiritually.  Reaching out to all ages, we offer a state-of-the-art exercise facility and locker rooms, group fitness classes, four Upward Sports for children, and various adult sports leagues all encompassed within the energetic, Christ-focused atmosphere of our church.

Check out our Warren Sports and Fitness Center Walk Thru Video below for a virtual tour of our amazing facility.

Still have questions? Call us and we will gladly give you a personal tour, 706.860.0454.

Joe Matosian

Joe Matosian

Joe is an NASM Certified Personal Trainer with a B.S. in Kinesiology from the University of Maryland.  His specialties include weight loss, cardio, balance and core training. He can design a personalized exercise program to meet almost any fitness goal.  Joe enjoys long distance running and is an experienced marathon competitor.

He is a member of the National Academy of Sports Medicine, American College of Sports Medicine, and IDEA Health & Fitness Association.  Joe also holds a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and works for the Department of  Defense.

Audra Lockman

Audra Lockman

Audra believes that regardless of your goal, a good strong base can lead you to your short and long term goals. Audra is newly certified with AFAA, but has been working with personal trainers for 6+ years. After an experience with debilitating back pain, she devoted much of her time to health and fitness and decided to turn it into a career in the hopes that she can share what she’s learned with others.

Brooke Ferguson

Brooke Ferguson

Brooke is an ACSM Certified Personal Trainer with a B.S. in Exercise Science. She specializes in women's strength training, weight loss, elderly, youth, and pre and postnatal training. Brooke also teaches group exercise classes and loves to help people find the joy in a healthy exercise program. Brooke loves to run and believes strength training should be vital a part of everyone's exercise routine.
